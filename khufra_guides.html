TIPS & TRICKS
  1. ATTACK WITH YOUR PASSIVE OFTEN - it is the only recover skill of Khufra. The enhanced basic attack grants him a strong poke abilities. Just attack with your passive whenever it is off cooldown to regenerate HP on the battlefield.
  2. SAVE SKILL 1 FOR INITIATING - it's the main skill for Khufra to join teamfights and the longer he charges it, the longer the knock-up effect will be. Be mindful whenever you want to use it as this being in cooldown can decide whether you win a teamfight or not.
  3. COVER YOUR TEAM WITH SKILL 2 - his skill 2 knocks back enemies whenever they use blink skills (Fanny's cable, Flicker, Harith's Dash, etc.) across Khufra. You can save your teammates by block their blink skills towards your teammates by using this skill.
