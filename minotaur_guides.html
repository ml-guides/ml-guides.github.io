TIPS & TRICKS
  1. GETTING RAGE MODE - hitting enemies with your attacks and skill 1 increases Minotaur's rage as well as taking attacks from enemies, minions, and jungle creeps. 
  2. MAINTAIN HIGH LEVELS OF RAGE - having high level of rage but not yet in rage gives you less time to activate rage mode during teamfights. Always do this so that you can tank enemies better with the passive boost of rage and cast your ult whenever you need to.
  3. MAXIMIZE MOTIVATION ROAR USAGE - this skill allows Minotaur to heal himself and his allies. It is very useful during teamfights to help your teammates survive longer and always use it whenever it is off cooldown since it has no mana or energy requirement so don't be shy to keep using it.
