TIPS & TRICKS
  1. ALWAYS HAVE THE BLUE BUFF - Ling is super dependent on the blue buff to be strong as it allows him to use skills more often with less energy requirement.
  2. USE SKILL 1 TO GANK ENEMIES - Ling is has high mobility because of this skill which allows him to walk on top of walls. Using this effectively helps you farm fast and escape ganks from your enemies. It is also his main initiating skill and it allows him to scout areas giving you and your teammates information about the enemy.
  3. TIME THE USAGE OF YOUR ULT - with the right timing, Ling can avoid massive damage from his enemies then you can CC them and burst them with the 4 field eyes that reset Defiant Sword cooldown and restore Lightness Points.
