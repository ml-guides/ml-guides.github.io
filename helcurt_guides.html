TIPS & TRICKS
  1. MAXIMIZE UTILITY OF YOUR PASSIVE - a very sick passive skill which allows Helcurt to silence enemies whenever they cast a skill that damages him. Skills far from Helcurt does not trigger the passive though.
  2. HIGH MOBILITY WITH SKILL 1 - a skill with super high mobility. Using in the early game allows Helcurt to move through walls to speed up jungling. Using in the middle-late game makes it a good initiating skill or escape mechanism.
  3. MAINTAIN MAX STACKS - keeping your skill 2 to max stacks makes you ready to gank or initiate teamfights so always have max stacks before trying to kill your enemies.
