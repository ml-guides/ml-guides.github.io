tips and tricks
  1. ROAM AS MUCH AS POSSIBLE - Chou is a great disabler while also being a bit tanky which makes him a great initiator. Roaming as soon as possible and getting your ult quick gives you and your team a chance to gank your enemies and getting free kills. 
  2. TARGET DAMAGE DEALERS - Chou can quickly get in behind enemy lines so it is easy to get close to enemy damage dealers and use your ultimate to bring them closer to your team and pick out the enemy cores.
  3. TRY NOT TO SOLO - Chou is the backbone of every teamfight, his skills are great in CC'ing and this advantage is best utilized by sticking to your team. It is not always guaranteed Chou can get a solo kill and most of the time, he needs follow-up damage on his CC's from his teammates to secure a kill.
  4. DODGING PROJECTILE CC's - His skill 2, Shunpo can make him immune from any disables while dashing. If you can master the timing, you can evade almost all projectile CC's from your enemies.
  5. HERO LOCK MODE FOR ULT - To properly kick your desired enemy target with your ult, you can enable the Hero Lock Mode in the settings so that you can lock on the enemy hero you want to use your ult with.
